LIVE

Battery & signal

Every connected device with its battery percentage, RSSI signal, and connection state. Auto-refresh from 1 s to 30 s.

RINGS

Battery donut

A donut chart with one ring per connected device, colored to match the row it belongs to. Distinct at a glance.

DEEP

Device detail

Click any row to open a full page with metrics, services list, vendor / class info, and connect / trust / block / remove actions.

SCAN

Discover

Radar view that streams AdapterEvent from BlueZ. Nearby devices appear sorted by RSSI. Pair on the spot.

TRAY

StatusNotifier

Tray icon via ksni. Tooltip lists connected devices with battery. Menu can toggle the window, refresh, or quit.

THEMES

Four palettes, four fonts

Each theme flips background, corner radius, and hero-number font family. Not just recolors — distinct identities.
